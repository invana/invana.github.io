Use Cases

Few ways in which Invana can be part of your journey to towards Innovations.

Web Automation

Automate anything on the web with browsers. Post data or crawl and fetch structured information from any a single webpage or from a pool of websites.

Rapid Prototyping

Invana GraphQL API's gives you instant APIs without coding, giving you power to rapidly experiment with your data schemas that work for you.

Run Microservices on stream

Invana gives you easy and potential to run microservices that range from simple text or image processing functions to Machine Learning models on your static data or streams.

Build Knowledge Graphs

Invana let's you analyse, compute and do operatoins on your stream data to produce knowledge and facts needed for your Machine Learning Projects.

Instantly Build Search APIs

Invana let's you crawl through data available on the internet or from your data buckets or databases and gives a scalable search API.
